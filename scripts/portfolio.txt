  # 距离检测 场景切换
     Distance detection scene switching

  利用 Arduino 超声波传感器发送超声波脉冲，并自动检测是否有
  信号返回，随后，用 pulseln() 函数获取到测距，当人与装置的
  距离大于 20cm 时，场景从眼球切换至常规房间场景。

  The Arduino ultrasonic sensor is used to send ultrasonic
  pulses and automatically detect whether a signal is returned.
  Subsequently, the pulseln() function is used to obtain the
  distance measurement, and when the distance between the
  person and the device is greater than 20cm, the scene is
  switched from the eye to the regular room scene.

   1        产品概述
            Product Overview

   “走之 Hiland”是一款为足不能出户，却依旧心怀旅游理想的人们设计的一款 app。
   本产品地图地点为节点，媒介，用户可以在地图上选择自己心仪的地点一探究竟，欣赏美景；也可以在推荐页
   面随意浏览，一睹感兴趣的风景名胜。不仅仅通过图文和视频，“走之 Hiland”更通过“带你直播看风景”
   的方式打造自己的核心竞争力，以此来为用户提供更加随心，更加具有沉浸感的线上旅行体验。

   "Go Hiland" is an app designed for people who can't leave home but still have the ideal of traveling.
   With the map location as the node and the medium, users can choose their favorite location
   on the map to explore and enjoy the beautiful scenery; they can also browse through the
   recommended pages to see the scenic spots they are interested in. Not only through graphics
   and videos, "Go Hiland" also builds its core competitiveness by "taking you live to see the
   scenery", so as to provide users with a more casual and more immersive online travel experience.

1        项目背景
         Project Background

2022 年是中国动画诞生百年。为了讲述中国动画百年成就，2022 年末，国家图书馆社会教育部依托“国图讲坛”，与中
国传媒大学动画学院合作举办了“妙笔生画 绘动时代——致敬中国动画百年”系列讲座（5 场）。
为进一步宣传推广动画艺术，更好地传播弘扬中华优秀传统文化，推动图书馆阅读资源走进高校读者群体，营造馆校共建的
良好阅读氛围，国家图书馆社会教育部和中国传媒大学动画学院拟在前期友好合作的基础上，集中优势资源和专业力量共建
共享，共同打造 2023“阅动文津 妙笔生画”读书季系列活动，以展览、讲座、读书会、社会实践等丰富立体的活动内容
服务广大师生和全国读者。

The year 2022 marks the centenary of the birth of Chinese animation. In order to tell the story of the
achievements of Chinese animation, the Social Education Department of the National Library, in cooperation with
the School of Animation of the Communication University of China, organized a series of lectures (5) in late 2022,
based on the "National Library Lecture Theatre", entitled "A Tribute to China In late 2002, the Social Education
Department of the National Library of China, in cooperation with the School of Animation of the Communication
University of China, held a series of lectures (5) entitled "The Art of Animation: A Tribute to China's Centennial".
To further promote the art of animation, better disseminate and promote the excellent Chinese traditional culture,
promote the library reading resources into the university readers, and create a good reading atmosphere for the
library and the university, the Social Education Department of the National Library and the School of Animation
of the Communication University of China intend to build on the basis of the previous friendly cooperation, pool
the advantageous resources and professional strength to jointly build and share the 2023 The series of activities
of "Read and Draw" reading season will serve teachers and students and readers nationwide with rich three-
dimensional activities such as exhibitions, lectures, reading sessions and social practice.

   # 主题解析
      Theme Analysis




   一代代中国动画人，传承匠心精神，不断守正创新，创作出一部部优秀动画作品，铸就了巍巍百年中国动画史。
   中国动画跌宕起伏的发展史与新中国不断发展密切相关，在新时代的背景下，中国动画更是紧扣时代精神，用
   当代的语言诠释着中国悠悠五千年的文化底蕴和独树一帜的东方世界观，真正做到了文化输出，展示文化自信。
   在世界舞台上展现了中国动画的独特风采，讲好了一个个动人的“中国故事”。

   Generations of Chinese animators have inherited the spirit of craftsmanship and innovation,
   creating outstanding animation works and forging a century of Chinese animation history. The
   history of Chinese animation is closely related to the continuous development of new China.
